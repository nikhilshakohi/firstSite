TECHTIPS

There are various ways to use the Internet in this world. Some of them are posted here:

1) You can download any Youtube video in the browser by adding 'ss' before youtube in the link and select the desired resolution and go for it.
Eg.To download a video, press 'ss' as follows: https://www.ssyoutube.com/watch?v=Io0fBr1XBUA and pressing enter
This is completely legal copyrighted by saveFrom.net

2) Never clear the recent apps in your phone. This will slow down your phone by a small amount as killing the recent apps will require more battery when you try to open the app again!
PS: External apps like cleanMaster will do the same thing to provide you additional RAM but it does drain your battery, so avoid them!

3) Ctrl+T opens a new tab and Ctrl+W closes corresponding tab in Chrome and finally;
Ctrl+Shift+T reopens an accidentally closed tab in Chrome!

4) If your pc is hanged or stopped, you can directly open Task manager by pressing CTRL+SHIFT+ESC keys together!
[instead of ctrl+alt+del and selecting task manager things]
